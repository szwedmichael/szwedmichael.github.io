Why we built this

I was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes in my early teens. After years of spreadsheets, manual carb counting, and constant guesswork, I realized the tools available to us weren't built to learn from our actual data.

Every body is different. Pizza spikes me for hours, but pasta barely moves the needle. A morning coffee hits differently than an afternoon one. Yesterday's run changes how I respond to everything today. None of my tools captured that.

So I built Sugarlytics -- an app that connects to your CGM and shows you, meal by meal, exactly what's happening. No more guessing. Just your data, your patterns, your answers.

Michael Szwed Founder, years old, living with T1D for years
