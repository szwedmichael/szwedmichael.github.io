How It Works

Step 1: Download the App

Start by downloading the Sugarlytics app from the App Store or Google Play. Our intuitive and user friendly design makes it easy to get started on your journey to better diabetes management.

Step 2: Create Your Account

Set up your personalized account by entering your basic details. This ensures your data is secure and allows you to sync across devices seamlessly.

Step 3: Connect Your Existing Tools

Easily connect your Continuous Glucose Monitor (CGM), health apps, fitness trackers, and other relevant devices. Sugarlytics integrates all your health data into one comprehensive platform, giving you a complete picture.

Step 4: Start Logging Your Meals

Begin logging your meals and insulin doses. The app tracks your food intake and insulin responses while also taking into account factors like time of day, fitness activity, and other relevant data to provide a comprehensive understanding of how different foods and conditions affect your blood sugar.

Step 5: Let Sugarlytics Learn and Optimize

Over time, our adaptive technology analyzes your data to learn your unique biology and patterns. Sugarlytics will optimize your insulin dosing recommendations, helping you achieve better blood sugar control with less guesswork.
